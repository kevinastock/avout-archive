Eleventh Sconic Cloister

The Eleventh Sconic Cloister is a cloister associated with the numbered Sconic Discipline within the Concent of Saunt Edhar. It is situated next to the local Edharian facilities; a shared lawn lies between the Edharian and Eleventh Sconic cloisters and is used informally by avout and servitors to sit, talk, and watch the sky.

  • Context and first mention: It is referenced as a landmark when a small group settled on the grass between the Edharian and Eleventh Sconic cloisters during a night of planned observation.
  • Affiliation: The cloister belongs to the Sconic community (Sconism) and functions as one of the concentâ€™s named cloisters.
  • Role and features: As a Cloister, it provides the customary sheltered arcade around a garden and serves as a quiet thoroughfare linking nearby halls.
  • Nearby: The Edharian Chapter house and chalk halls are close by, with easy movement from indoor work to the shared lawn outside.

Status: Active within the concent; no restrictions beyond normal Discipline customs are noted.

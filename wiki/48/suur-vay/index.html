Suur Vay

Suur Vay is a member of the Valers, the cohort linked with Ringing Vale disciplines. She is present at the Concent of Saunt Tredegarh during the large gathering of avout and is identified among the Valers assigned to depart with a small evacuation cell.

Role and context

During the organized dispersal from Tredegarh, Vay takes a seat with a Valer cohort that includes Fraa Osa, Suur Esma, and Fraa Gratho. Before departure, the Valers pay formal respects to Fraa Jad. When Lio boards and assumes charge of the cell, the Valers acknowledge his lead.

Preparation at Elkhazg

After dispersal from Tredegarh, Vay travels with the cell to Elkhazg. During the flight and subsequent stay she studies space suit and mission materials alongside Fraa Jesry, trading documents and taking part in hands-on practice for individual suit-and-stage operations aimed at reaching the Daban Urnud. In briefings she emphasizes disciplined use of liquid oxygen and asks pointed questions about the life-support "tender" that stores air and fuel and recycles carbon dioxide scrubbers. When Fraa Jad demonstrates a fresh Teglon tiling in the Decagon there, the Valers bow to him; Vay is among that cohort.

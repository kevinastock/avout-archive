Norslof

Overview

Norslof is a small city in the far north, reached after crossing a mountain pass. For parties heading deeper into the cold regions, it functions as a practical waystation and logistics hub. A traveling company searching for Fraa Orolo descended into Norslof, stopped at an outlying drummon (heavy freight vehicle) refueling station, and made runs into town for gear and repairs before pushing north.

Notable Features

  • Drummon refueling station several miles outside the commercial center, with a mess hall, ceiling-mounted screens showing synchronized speely (video recording) feeds, rooms for rent, and permission to sleep in vehicles.
  • A spacious back lot used for vehicle work; visitors installed hobnailed winter tires, replaced coolants and lubricants with low-temperature formulations, and repacked equipment for deep-cold travel.
  • City shops supplying extreme-cold gear, including suitsacks (cold-weather garment) and other practical items for over-ice journeys. Traffic can be heavy, but experienced locals know economical sources and improvisations.

Bolt

First Appearance and Context

The bolt is observed in everyday mathic life as a long, rectangular cloth worn by Avout. It is used for hooding and modesty while moving through workrooms and cloisters, and comes up in conversation where an elder enumerates ‚Äúthe bolt, the chord, and the sphere‚Äù during a library exchange.

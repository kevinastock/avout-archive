Decenarian Math

First Appearance and Context

The Decenarian Math is a walled precinct within the Concent of Saunt Edhar, home to the Tenners (Decenarians) at this concent. Community life here centers on work and rites in the Mynster, with the great clock and its mechanisms providing the daily rhythm.

Current Status

Active within the concent. The Decenarian Math continues its clock and bell work and participates in rites in the Mynster; following the later Voco, many Tenner members left under formal summons while remaining residents maintained ordinary duties.

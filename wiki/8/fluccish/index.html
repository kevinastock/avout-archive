Fluccish

Fluccish is the everyday vernacular used outside the walls of the maths. It is contrasted with Orth, the language maintained within the mathic world. Avout may switch to Fluccish when addressing visitors from beyond the walls so they are better understood.

First noted usage

Fluccish is highlighted during an interview in the New Library, when Fraa Erasmas chooses to speak it out of consideration for an artisan from outside the walls, and Fraa Orolo likewise switches to Fluccish while questioning the visitor. The contrast with Orth is made explicit in their conversation.

Relationship to Orth and the mathic world

Within the maths, Orth (with its historical forms) is the customary language for scholarship and internal discourse. The exchange between avout and visitors shows how Orth terms can sound quaint in Fluccish conversation and vice versa.

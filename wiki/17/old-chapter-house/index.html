Old Chapter House

The Old Chapter House is the historic chapter house of the Edharian Order within the Concent of Saunt Edhar. Despite its name, it is not a separate building but a broadened portion of the cloister gallery, used infrequently except for ceremonies and quiet order business. It is remembered as the heart of the order and associated by tradition with Saunt Edhar.

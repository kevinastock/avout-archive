Definition and Standing

A Perelithian Liaison is a permanent union between one Fraa and one Suur. Within the Concent of Saunt Edhar, it is the most serious recognized liaison and is treated as equivalent to marriage. In general mathic usage, it is a specific kind of Liaison governed by the Discipline and local customs.

Procedures and Publication

Per mathic practice at Saunt Edhar, liaisons are to be announced (‚Äúpublished‚Äù) when formed and when dissolved, both to curb gossip and to formalize obligations. Comparisons and Related Types

Accounts distinguish multiple liaison types of varying seriousness. At Saunt Edhar, Tivian is the least serious, Etrevanean lies between, and Perelithian is the most serious. Other named types exist in mathic lore, including episodic forms that meet only during decennial openings.

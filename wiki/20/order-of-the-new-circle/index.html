Order of the New Circle

First Appearance and Context

The Order of the New Circle is cited among avout as a coeval current with the Reformed Old Faanians. It is later named in full when Fraa Corlandin speaks with a Tenner in the Refectory ahead of Eliger. In local recollection at Saunt Edhar, after the Third Sack many Edharians were relocated to make room for a New Circle and a Reformed Old Faanite chapter. In the period following the close of Apert, repeated Eliger auts add several members from the Tenner cohort to the New Circle, including Ala and Lio.

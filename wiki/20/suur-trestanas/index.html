Suur Trestanas

First Appearance and Context

Suur Trestanas is first observed enforcing Discipline inside the cloistered precincts of the Concent of Saunt Edhar, issuing minor penances and presiding from the Regulant’s place during services in the Mynster. Near the opening, she was seen at the starhenge cupola with the Master of the Keys; after portcullises were drawn shut, she had a tense, private exchange with Fraa Orolo by the gate. From that point the Starhenge remained sealed, an extraordinary situation noted by avout across the concent.

Roles/Actions and Affiliations

  • As the Warden Regulant, Trestanas enforces the Discipline within the maths, oversees conduct around visitors and instruments, and assigns penances when duties are neglected.
  • Affiliation: Order of the New Circle. Within this order she is regarded as a central figure whose views circulate quickly among peers.
  • During and after the public opening, when visiting Inquisitors were present, she "threw" The Book at a young fraa with a sentence reaching Chapter Five, effectively placing him in seclusion in a Regulant court cell. He later inferred that the timing kept him away from the community while the Inquisition was among them.
  • After a formal Evocation, she declined to escalate penalty for his brief absence from his cell, and clarified that if such an aut occurred again he was to view it from behind the Primate’s screen and then return. She treated attendance at Voco as an exception to cell confinement.
  • During the lead-up to a rare aut that shook the community, she was seen moving between offices in the Regulant court. When she encountered a tenner ascending with drawing equipment, she assessed the situation and let him pass without challenge.

Relationships

  • Fraa Orolo: engaged in a tense, muffled exchange at the starhenge portcullis; the encounter coincided with the starhenge’s closure.
  • Fraa Erasmas: recipient of her Book sentence; later she showed measured leniency regarding a procedural lapse during Evocation and provided explicit guidance on conduct. After a later aut, he harbors dark suspicions that Trestanas may have had an unseen role in what befell a senior theor; this reflects his perception rather than an established fact.
  • Fraa Corlandin: a continuing liaison is assumed by observers, which can make encounters socially delicate.
  • Fraa Spelikon: noted by avout as having been passed over for the office in favor of Trestanas.
  • Visiting Inquisitors (including Onali and Varax): her disciplinary decisions and communications took place while they were on site.

Descriptions/Characteristics

Trestanas is brisk, organized, and exacting—a new holder of office who initially issued many small penances. She distinguishes carefully between what avout must do versus want to do when assigning penance. In practice she can be pragmatic and even lenient once circumstances change (e.g., after visiting authorities depart). Among peers she has a watchful reputation; jokes about her listening from the leaf piles reflect heightened vigilance during the starhenge closure. In the same period described above, some avout remarked that she seemed to be “cracking” and quipped that she might be sent to Physicians’ Commons—comments that reflect tension and rumor rather than any confirmed diagnosis.

Current Status/Location

Active as Warden Regulant among the hierarchs, focused on maintaining order during the season of Eliger and the rare Evocation, while restrictive measures remain in effect around the starhenge. She remains visibly engaged in the Regulant court’s operations during and after a later aut.

Summary:

Suur Trestanas is a suur who serves as the Warden Regulant at the Concent of Saunt Edhar, enforcing the Discipline. Around and after Apert she imposed Book penance that kept a young fraa sequestered while Inquisitors visited, and she was present near the subsequent closure of the starhenge.

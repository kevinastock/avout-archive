DynaZoom

DynaZoom is mentioned by an artisan from outside the walls while explaining why a colleague was not allowed to record a speely of Provener inside the Mynster. According to that account, the colleague’s speelycaptor—equipped with Eagle‑Rez, SteadiHand, and DynaZoom—was said to be capable of seeing across into other parts of the Mynster, even through screens, and so permission was denied.

First appearance and context

  • Introduced during a discussion in the New Library concerning whether a speely could be made of the rite at Provener inside the Mynster. The capability was cited again during a Regulant inquiry into that episode.

Characteristics and associations

  • Treated as a proper‑named feature paired with other branded capabilities (Eagle‑Rez and SteadiHand) on speelycaptors.
  • In an in‑math explanation of the marketing terms, the trio was characterized functionally as: a zoom/magnification feature (DynaZoom), a way to detect faint images against a noisy or low‑light background (Eagle‑Rez), and image stabilization to correct hand shake (SteadiHand).

Notable claims

  • The claimed combined effect was to image beyond publicly visible areas inside the Mynster, even through bright screens at a distance. This was presented as the reason given for restricting the recording attempt.

Further mentions

  • Later invoked metaphorically by an avout while being discharged from a Laterran hospital aboard the Daban Urnud, likening himself to Artisan Flec’s speelycaptor “with Eagle‑Rez, SteadiHand, and DynaZoom,” reinforcing the term’s use as a named zoom/magnification feature.

Status

  • Mentioned but not shown in use within the math; no maker, specifications, or independent description are provided.
Summary:

A named zoom/magnification capability on an extramuros speelycaptor, cited alongside Eagle-Rez and SteadiHand. Its inclusion—combined with those features—was given as a reason to block recording of Provener inside the Mynster.

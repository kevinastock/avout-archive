Saunt Tancred's Nebula

First appearance and context

The nebula enters the narrative when an avout is assigned a problem by Fraa Orolo that uses a photo-mnemonic tablet bearing its image. The exercise is set on the eve of Apert within the Concent of Saunt Edhar, during a mealtime conversation in the Refectory and nearby kitchen. The same tablet is also seen in a workroom of the Starhenge, resting among the avout's notes.

Role and function

Saunt Tancred's Nebula serves as a study image for questions about stellar processes, specifically the formation of heavy nuclei in the cores of stars.

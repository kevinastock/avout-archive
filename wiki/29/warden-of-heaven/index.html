Warden of Heaven

First Appearance and Context

Outside the walls, the Warden of Heaven is referenced as a source of guidance distributed in casts and is sometimes treated by extras as the name of an ark. Avout encountering such claims describe it as an outsider phenomenon and deny any institutional connection to the maths.

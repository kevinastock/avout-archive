Erasmas

Overview

A Decenarian avout from the Concent of Saunt Edhar, Evoked under Voco, he assumes practical leadership within a Peregrin cohort. He directs a detour to Bly's Butte to look for Fraa Orolo before continuing toward the Concent of Saunt Tredegarh. Upon reaching the village of Samble and ascending the butte with companions, he finds Orolo’s simple cell and notes; guided by Fraa Jad, he helps secure and burn the notes and learns that Orolo has gone north. While traveling extramuros he is often addressed by the familiar name "Raz," especially by Cord, and he characterizes himself as "technically a Feral" for choosing the detour.

Appearance and Traits

Erasmas is conscientious and pragmatic, quick to organize travel and keep companions safe. He balances respect for the Discipline with extramuros realities and is comfortable working in multiple tongues. He shows curiosity and quick uptake in theorics discussions, while deferring to elders when evidence calls for it. He also presents as reflective and self‑aware, sometimes revising personal letters multiple times, and shows a practical turn when equipping a journey.

Roles and Actions

  • Convoy organization and planning: Helps arrange vehicles, seating, and wayfinding for a mixed company while keeping the detour and onward travel in view.
  • Detour and ascent of Bly’s Butte: Hikes with Lio and Fraa Criscan from Samble up the butte, identifying a homemade telescope and a secluded, roofless cloister used as Orolo’s cell. Inside, he notes phototypes and hand‑drawn leaves diagramming names and connections across centuries.
  • Securing Orolo’s materials: After Estemard arrives (briefly disarmed by Lio), Erasmas supports a cautious response. At Jad’s direction he helps remove Orolo’s leaves and burn them in the brazier; he retains several phototypes depicting a large man‑made excavation for later review.
  • Theoric dialogue en route: During the climb he engages with Fraa Criscan’s calca on “Complex Protism,” reflecting on his namesake Erasmas at Saunt Baritoe and the idea of information flowing among multiple cosmi.
  • Direction to go north: After descending, Jad privately instructs him to go north to find Orolo. Erasmas agrees, despite concern about Discipline penalties, trusting that Jad will advocate on his behalf at the gathering.
  • Northbound logistics: purchases fuel, food, warm clothing, tents, and other gear for the mountain drive using his money card and funds available via Fraa Jad’s card.
  • On the road: rides with Cord while a companion’s fetch leads; camps at a tarn, starts a fire, and helps organize new equipment before continuing into higher country.
  • Communication: writes a letter to Ala, ultimately producing a short, direct version by firelight after several discarded drafts.
  • Northbound objective: participates in a small party heading into the mountains to seek signs of Orolo, expecting cold conditions and possible camping.

Relationships

  • Fraa Orolo: Mentor and object of the current search; Erasmas explores Orolo’s cell on the butte and preserves select phototypes at Jad’s request.
  • Fraa Jad: Senior traveling elder whose guidance Erasmas follows—especially in handling Orolo’s notes and in the decision to go north.
  • Lio and Fraa Criscan: Close companions on the ascent; Lio subdues Estemard during a brief scuffle, and Criscan provides the Complex Protism calca.
  • Estemard: Former Edharian associated with the butte; encountered living among Deolaters. Interaction is tense but resolves without injury.
  • Cord: Close traveling companion; she uses "Raz" as his familiar name, teases him into keeping his letter simple, and shares the driving. He refers to her as his "sib."
  • Sammann: Travels in tandem and works his jeejah when possible; intends to try tracking Orolo’s trail.
  • Ganelial Crade: Drives another fetch and tends to take the lead on routes and camp setup; he is wary of Crade’s possible outside obligations but follows his lead when balanced by Sammann’s oversight.
  • Ala: Someone with whom he recently began a relationship before her Evocation; he chooses to contact her by letter rather than attempting to untangle events in detail.

Current Status

Traveling north into the mountains with companions after departing Samble; has camped at a tarn and resumed the drive. He remains focused on the search for Orolo and regards himself as Feral for having chosen this detour.

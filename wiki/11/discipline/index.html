Discipline

First Appearance and Context

The Discipline is referenced as the code shaping possessions and attire for the avout during preparations for a Decennial Apert at the Concent of Saunt Edhar. It is described as originating with people who lived in warm places and, among other rules, as allowing each avout to own a bolt, a chord, and a sphere, while saying nothing explicitly about footwear.

First appearance and context

In discussion at Saunt Edhar, Protan forms are proposed as a neutral ground for communicating with unknown outsiders: if two cultures can each express the same theoric truth, the shared form might bridge notational and linguistic gaps. This usage places the forms as a practical touchstone for proof and communication inside the Mathic World.

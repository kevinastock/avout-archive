Decennial Apert

First Appearance and Context

Decennial Apert is observed at sunrise inside the Mynster, where avout gather for a music-led rite synchronized to the great clock. On the first morning of a new decade, coded bell changes announce the opening and a processional specific to the day is sung.   • Closely linked to the Decenarians and their math, to the time-gated portals (especially the Decade and Year Gates), and to the ceremonial functions of the Mynster within the Concent of Saunt Edhar.

Descriptions/Characteristics

  • Signaling and music: bell-rung changes cast from the belfry precede the rite; a processional unique to the decennial opening is sung.
  • Timed actuation: at sunrise, refracted light enters the hall as the cube and octahedron weights descend on rails to open the Year and Decade gates; the assembly often responds with audible emotion as the weights slow and come to rest just above the chancel floor.
  • Attire and markers: avout wear formal wraps; members of the Decenarian cohort may make their spheres glow in a scarlet hue so they can be distinguished in mixed groups.

Current Status/Location

Most recently witnessed within the Concent of Saunt Edhar: at sunrise, the mechanisms operated smoothly and the Year and Decade gates opened together as part of Decennial Apert, initiating the brief period of regulated contact associated with Apert.

Summary:

A decennial observance within the mathic world when the Decade Gate opens at sunrise, accompanied by a music-led rite and the concurrent opening of the Year Gate. It is the ten-year instance of Apert and briefly permits regulated contact between the math and extramuros.

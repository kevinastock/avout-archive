Avout

First Appearance and Context

Avout are those who live within a Math under the Cartasian Discipline, distinct from the world Extramuros. The word is used by insiders and outsiders to mark that boundary. During Apert, gates open on a regulated cycle and brief, supervised contact is permitted.

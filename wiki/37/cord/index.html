Cord

Overview

Cord is an extramuros (outside math walls) machinist and the sib (close companion) of Fraa Erasmas. She provides hands-on driving, repairs, and route-minded logistics for the company pursuing Fraa Orolo, favoring fixes and gear that work in the field.

Appearance and Traits

Direct, capable, and pragmatic. She thinks in terms of tools, parts, and procedures; prefers machines that can be disassembled and repaired; and stays steady under pressure. Plain-spoken, with dry humor and an affectionate bluntness toward close companions. On Ecba she keeps tools close at hand (e.g., long-nosed pliers) and is candid about unease when a place feels wrong, pushing for a clear plan.

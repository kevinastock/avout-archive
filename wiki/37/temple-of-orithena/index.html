Temple of Orithena

The Temple of Orithena is an ancient ceremonial complex at Orithena, remembered for a vast domed hall with an oculus acting as a camera obscura. At midday, a sunbeam projected through the oculus traced an analemma across the floor, forming the climax of the rite later echoed in mathic practice.

Notable Features

  • Domed hall with a central oculus functioning as a camera obscura, producing a seasonal analemma on the floor
  • A named decagonal forecourt before the temple, known as The Decagon
  • Traditional linkage to the classical tiling problem called The Teglon

History

  • The south slope of Ecba's volcano exploded and collapsed in -2621, burying the Temple of Orithena under ash and rubble and obliterating a harbor on the island's southeastern coast once used by early physiologers.

Associations

  • Part of the broader complex referred to as the Halls of Orithena
  • The temple and its rites are emblematic touchstones in classical theorics and liturgy

Recent Activity

  • A large, orderly excavation on Ecba aims to uncover the buried temple. The site presents a dark perimeter wall and switchback access road; visitors encounter an avout-run checkpoint at the base and are not permitted to drive farther. Avout at the checkpoint wear bolts and chords openly and sell small items to visitors (e.g., soap made with volcanic ash). Independent observers on the Reticulum attribute the effort to an Edharian Order spinoff drawing personnel from multiple Edharian maths; the work appears long-running and predominantly manual, with little heavy machinery evident. Recognition of exposed floor markings resembling the temple's analemma helped confirm the identification.

Status/Access

The original complex is long destroyed and largely buried by ancient ash; present-day access is via the remote excavation compound on Ecba. Public access is restricted; checkpoint staff turn back vehicles at the lower road. Governance and formal visitation policy are not specified.

Summary:

An ancient ceremonial complex at Orithena noted for a domed oculus and a solar rite. Buried by a catastrophic eruption in -2621, it is now the focus of an ongoing math-run excavation on Ecba that many sources credit to the Edharian Order.

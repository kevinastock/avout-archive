Criscan

Overview

Fraa Criscan is a Hundreder avout of the Concent of Saunt Edhar. As part of a small Peregrin detour to seek Orolo, he rides with companions and then hikes from the village of Samble toward the summit of Bly’s Butte with Fraa Erasmas and Lio. Earlier he relayed the group’s working label “the Cousins” for the visitors’ craft and offered straightforward rotation estimates; on the ascent he turns discussion into a calca about polycosmic models, reflecting Edharian interests.

Appearance and Traits

  • Identified as an Edharian Hundreder; described as the youngest of the Hundreders present in the traveling party.
  • Analytical and precise: comfortable with back‑of‑the‑leaf calculations and quick to frame ideas as a calca; careful about terms like “cosmos” versus “causal domain.”
  • Matter‑of‑fact and somewhat literal in tone; focuses on what seems relevant to current aims.

Roles and Actions

  • Peregrin travel and hike: Walks the road from Samble up toward the top of Bly’s Butte with Erasmas and Lio while the rest of the group remains below.
  • Calca on Complex Protism: On a silted stretch of road he sketches a directed, acyclic diagram to explain a “Complex” variant of Protism, in which information may percolate one‑way through multiple cosmi, rather than a simple two‑box picture.
  • Cited in a later messal: During a dinner dialog on polycosmic models, another avout redraws the same diagram families—nicknamed the Freight Train, the Firing Squad, and the Wick—explicitly recalling Criscan’s road calca as the example.
  • Teacher–fid link: States that he was the fid of Fraa Paphlagon; he asks after Orolo’s reputation and compares Paphlagon’s.
  • Rumors and later recollection: Acknowledges talk about a long‑standing tradition called The Lineage focused on the Teglon; some critics use it to frame accusations about Edharian priorities. The narration later recalls Criscan’s account in that sense, preserving it as rumor and debate rather than settled fact.
  • At Orolo’s cloister: After the group enters a small, improvised math where Orolo had lived, Criscan later departs with Estemard toward a village picnic while others finish their business.

Relationships

  • Fraa Jad: Earlier rides near him during travel and shares analytic conversation about the visitor.
  • Fraa Paphlagon: Criscan’s former teacher; he identifies himself as Paphlagon’s fid and treats his work with respect.
  • Fraa Erasmas: Trades questions and calca talk during the hike, including comparisons of reputations (Orolo and Paphlagon).
  • Edharian Order: He is openly Edharian and engages with perceptions of that Order’s priorities when discussing the HTW and the rumored Lineage.

Current Status

Extramuros with the Peregrin company in the Samble/Bly’s Butte area. Last seen heading down with Estemard to join a local picnic, remaining part of the group’s ongoing search for Orolo.

Summary:

An Edharian Hundreder from the Concent of Saunt Edhar, the youngest among the Hundreders traveling in a small Peregrin company toward Bly’s Butte. He hikes with Erasmas and Lio, contributes practical estimates about the icosahedral craft (“the Cousins”), and gives an impromptu calca on Complex Protism, noting he was Fraa Paphlagon’s fid.

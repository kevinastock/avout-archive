First Appearance and Context

Cord is introduced around a decennial Apert at the Concent of Saunt Edhar, when she reconnects with her sib Fraa Erasmas outside the walls. She works at an old metalworks compound on a canal slip a short walk from the Century Gate. During the same opening, she is admitted as a supervised visitor to see the clockworks inside the Mynster. At the Tenth Night supper under the canopy, she attends with her boyfriend Rosk and their younger cousin Dath, joining conversations that compare everyday tasks with geometric reasoning.

Roles/Actions and Affiliations

  • Machinist and artisan: Operates heavy, precise machinery to produce complex metal parts and small crafted items; she has supplied pieces to the Ita.

  • Visitor and observer: Accompanies Erasmas to view mechanisms and upper works, asking practical questions and comparing design choices in terms of wear and reliability.
  • Machine-hall: Her machine-hall at the metalworks compound is cited as the place where Jesry and Erasmas encountered the Ita Sammann; during Apert she and Sammann were observed wearing very dark protective goggles to shield their eyes from an extremely bright arc.

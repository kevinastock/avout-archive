Inquisitors

First Appearance and Context

At the Concent of Saunt Edhar, two visitors were recognized as Inquisitors during services in the Mynster. Their presence coincided with heightened scrutiny, and soon after an aut of Voco they departed by aerocraft from the plaza beyond the Day Gate.

Roles and Affiliations

  • Officers of the Inquisition who travel among concents to keep practice uniform and to enforce the Discipline.
  • Act in concert with local hierarchs when directives are issued.

Important Actions (current period)

  • Following a broad directive to close every Starhenge, Inquisitors were sent to ensure that closures were carried out and observed.
  • At Saunt Edhar, visiting Inquisitors were present during rites and then departed after a subsequent Evocation.

Relationships

  • Named at Saunt Edhar: Varax and Onali.
  • Institutional: Work within the framework of the Inquisition alongside mathic hierarchs.

Descriptions/Characteristics

  • Public appearance: During rites, visiting Inquisitors have been seen in purple robes with distinctive hats; they may also move incognito among visitors before revealing their role.
  • Conduct: Treated by avout as a routinized, bureaucratic presence focused on inquiries, observation, and ensuring compliance with directives.

Current Status

Active across the maths. Dispatches to verify starhenge closures have been noted; no continuing presence at Saunt Edhar is recorded after the Evocation noted above.

Summary:

Roving officials of the Inquisition who inspect concents and enforce the Discipline. They have been observed at Saunt Edhar and dispatched to ensure compliance with orders such as closing starhenges.

Semantic Faculty of the Concent of Saunt Muncoster

First appearance and context

The Semantic Faculty is cited in an entry of The Dictionary (4th edition, dated A.R. 3000) that discusses Saunt Proc and contrasts two lineages at the Concent of Saunt Muncoster: the Syntactic Faculty under Proc and the Semantic Faculty under Saunt Halikaarn. In that account, orders are distinguished as originating from one faculty or the other following the Reconstitution.

Role and function

As presented so far, the Semantic Faculty is one of the named faculties of the Concent of Saunt Muncoster, set in contrast to the Syntactic Faculty. It is associated with orders said to originate from it and with the patronage of Saunt Halikaarn. Specific curriculum, practices, or internal organization have not yet been described.

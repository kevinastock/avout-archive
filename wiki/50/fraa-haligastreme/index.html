Fraa Haligastreme

First Appearance and Context

Fraa Haligastreme is first observed within the Concent of Saunt Edhar (mathic monastery) during Decennial Apert (decade-cycle public opening), when visitors enter under controlled conditions. He is approached by a visiting priest delivering a message to a small group; Haligastreme listens politely while casting significant glances toward nearby hierarchs, including Fraa Delrakhones and Statho.

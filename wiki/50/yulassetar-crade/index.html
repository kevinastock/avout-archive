Yulassetar Crade

Recent developments and context

  • After the evacuation from Orithena to a military-run beach camp nicknamed "New Orithena," Yul sits with Cord, Gnel, Sammann, and Erasmas as delegations bound for Convox (mass convocation of avout) arrive to secure evidence and testimony. Yul and Cord note that many people wish to ask them questions about what they saw, and they may travel to Tredegarh to provide accounts.
  • Engagement: Yul proposes to Cord with a plain metal ring he forged from a loop cut out of the Geometers' parachute rigging; he hammers it to size in a campfire. Cord announces that they are engaged, and the group celebrates. They speak of first retrieving Cord's fetch from Norslof and then taking a short trip into the mountains as a delayed honeymoon.
  • Rig status: Erasmas recalls the wreckage of Yul's rig flying past during the Orithena events; Yul does not comment, which suggests the rig was likely destroyed. This remains an inference from onlookers' remarks rather than a confirmed statement.

Relationships and affiliations (updates)

  • Cord: formerly described here as his partner; she is now his fiancee. They continue to act in close concert on practical tasks and decisions.
  • Ganelial Crade: cousin. When Gnel declares he will remain with the Orithenans, Yul responds warmly and toasts him at the beach encampment.

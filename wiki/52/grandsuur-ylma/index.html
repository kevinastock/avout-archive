Grandsuur Ylma

First Appearance and Context

During the opening of the gates at Apert, a woman from extramuros who recognized Grandsuur Ylma approached her at the Day Gate of the Concent of Saunt Edhar. The visitor explained that her late father had supported the concent and used to enter to attend lectures and read; the family brought a potted sapling to be transplanted on the grounds as a memorial.
